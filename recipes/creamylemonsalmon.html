Creamy Lemon Salmon

Healthy yet indulgent salmon recipe for the perfect week night meal.

This creamy salmon recipe is one you'll keep coming back to. It can be made in 20 minutes and keeps the family happy while being nutritious.

Ingredient

  • 1 pound fresh salmon
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • salt and pepper
  • flour for dredging
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth or white wine
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1/4 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • fresh parsley, chopped, to taste

Steps

  1. Cut your salmon into 4 pieces. Sprinkle with garlic powder and season with salt and pepper. Dredge each piece in flour on all sides.
  2. Add the olive oil and butter to skillet over medium-high heat. Add fish when hot. Cook for 3-4 minutes per side or until nicely browned. Be careful not to overcook - the salmon shouldn't be fully cooked yet. Remove from pan and set aside.
  3. Add the chicken broth, lemon juice, Italian seasoning, and Dijon mustard to the pan. Stir until mustard has dissolved, and let bubble for a minute or two.
  4. Add the cream to the pan, along with the salmon. Let it cook for a few more minutes until the sauce has thickened a bit and the fish has cooked through.
  5. Sprinkle with fresh parsley and serve immediately.
