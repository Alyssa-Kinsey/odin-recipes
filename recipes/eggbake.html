Egg Bake

An easy to make egg casserole with sausage, eggs, and cheese. Perfect for Christmas morning.

This egg casserole can be prepared in less than 30 minutes, and refrigerated for leftovers. The bell peppers and sausage add a delicious touch to the and are a healthy and satisfying way to start your day.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound pork sausage
  • 12 eggs
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • salt and pepper
  • 4 green onions
  • 1/2 green bell pepper, diced
  • 1/2 red bell pepper, diced
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Spray 9x13 pan with cooking spray.
  2. Combine eggs, sour cream, milk, cheese, salt, and pepper in a large bowl. Mix until just combined.
  3. Heat a large skillet over medium heat. Add sausage and cook until browned, breaking into small pieces as it cooks. Drain grease and to egg mixture.
  4. Add bell peppers and onion to same skillet and saute for 2-3 minutes. Add to bowl with eggs and stir to combine.
  5. Pour mixture into greased pan and bake for 40-45 minutes or until edges are set and center is just barely jiggly.
