Parmesan Risotto

This recipe will have you making what seems like a complicated dish in no time!

Risotto has been regarded as a delicious yet time-consuming meal to make. I'm here to break it down for you and make it easy to understand. It is made creamy and fulfilling with white wine and parmesan cheese.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 medium onion, diced finely
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 4 cups vegetable stock
  • 1/4 cup dry white wine
  • 1 cup arborio rice
  • 1 cup grated parmesan
  • 3 tablespoons chopped parsley

Steps

  1. Heat vegetable stock and keep warm.
  2. Add butter and oil to large skillet over medium heat.
  3. Add the onions and cook until just tender. Add the garlic and cook for one more minute.
  4. Add the rice and toss to coat completely.
  5. Add the wine and stir until it is absorbed.
  6. Add 1 ladle full of stock and stir until it absorbs.
  7. Repeat this until you have used almost all of the stock. This should take about 17-25 minutes.
  8. When you add the last ladle, also add the parsley, only let the stock absorb halfway and then add cheese.
  9. Let is absorb until it is creamy and thick, but not soupy.
  10. Serve, add extra parmesan if desired.
